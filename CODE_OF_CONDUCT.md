# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**

- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experience levels
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks of any kind
- Publishing private information without consent
- Any conduct that a reasonable person would consider inappropriate

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the maintainer at **cleber.demgoncalves@gmail.com**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly.

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.
